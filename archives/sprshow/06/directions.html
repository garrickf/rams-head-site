Memorial Auditorium is located on the Stanford Campus at 551 Serra Mall near the intersection of Serra Street and Galvez Street. Parking is free after 4pm. For a map including parking locations, visit the Parking & Trsportaiton Website.

From US-101, take the Embarcadero Rd. exit and go west. When you cross El Camino Real, Embarcadero Road will become Galvez Street. Parking is available near the intersection of Galvez and Memorial Way.

From I-280, take the Page Mill Rd. exit and go east. Turn left on El Camino Real, and then turn left again on Serra Street.
