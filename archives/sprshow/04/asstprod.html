Looking for Assistant Producers!

 Be on the production staff of Ram’s Head Theatrical Society’s Spring Musical!
…presenting Kiss Me Kate

Ram's Head encourages people of all backgrounds to apply

Deadline: Dec. 1st.  Theatrical experience not required.  Download an application!

Positions Available:

Business Manager – Monitors the budget with the producer; Organizes and oversees the various designers and costs of production; Organizes accounting (including receipts and reimbursements); Coordinates Stanford Fund for cast and staff of Kiss Me Kate; Finds corporate sponsors for Kiss Me Kate.  (Two positions available.)

Publicity Manager- In charge of programs, posters, flyers, press releases, media relations, and other creative publicity ideas; help Business Managers find corporate sponsors.  (Two positions available.  One position may be to maintain the Kiss Me Kate portion of Ram’s Head’s Website.)

 Ticketing Manager – In charge of ticket creation and box office.  Sells and helps establish the selling and distribution plan of tickets. 

 Housing Manager – Prepares Memorial Auditorium for the production, hires ushers, and organizes concessions for performances and rehearsals.

 Scheduling/Production Manager – Establishes rehearsal/meeting/casting schedules.  Reminds people of schedule and deals with scheduling problems.  Determines rehearsal/meeting locations and confirms availability.  Deals with rehearsal logistics such as script copying.

 Social Manager - Someone to plan and organize cast retreat, and cast party, along with events during spring break to give cast a break from build, will also serve as support to the other assistant producers.

 Program Manager – Works with financial manager to gather ads and puts together the program.  Will also work with graphics artists to design the cover of the program, and possibly the promotional posters.

 Crisis Manager – Works closely with producer to complete many various tasks concerning the production of Kiss Me Kate.  Of all the positions available, this one provides the most exposure to the ins and outs of being a producer.  Crisis Manager will also provide additional support to the other producers.
