Elton John and Tim Rice's AIDA is a contemporary Tony-Award-winning musical that explores a grand ancient-Egyptian tale of the love between a soldier and an enslaved princess – a love that intensifies the conflict between two warring nations and raises questions of duty, trust, and loyalty.  With a pop-rock score that features stirring ballads and rousing choral numbers, AIDA is a modern-day crowd pleaser that embraces multi-cultural casting and exuberant dancing, staging and singing.

Produced by Lisa Kerner
Directed by Mason Flink

Coming to Stanford's Memorial Auditorium
April 10, 11, 16, 17, & 18 at 8 PM
