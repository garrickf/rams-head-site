Founded in 1911, Ram’s Head Theatrical Society is a student-run theatrical group striving to provide the exciting and energetic productions of the highest quality to the entire bay area. Producer of the much loved Big Game Gaieties and the Original Winter One-Acts, Ram’s Head’s biggest theatrical endeavor is the Spring Musical.

Past Ram’s Head Spring Musicals have included West Side Story, A Chorus Line, Pippin, Into the Woods, Evita, Jesus Christ Superstar, Guys and Dolls, Company, Cabaret, and Thoroughly Modern Millie.
