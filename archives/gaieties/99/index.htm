Big Game Gaieties '99



Big Game Gaieties is Stanford's oldest, wildest, and most popular theatre tradition. It's a student-created musical extravaganza performed in anticipation of Stanford winning Big Game against Cal. Singing, dancing, and nudity are guaranteed. We'll even have appearances by special guests (this year is Casper's last Gaieties!).

When and Where: November 17, 18, 19 at 8 PM in Memorial Auditorium.
Tickets: $9 students (special coupon for one dollar off on Wed. night), $15 general.
Contact the producer if you have any questions about the show.
