"Lordy Gordy" (11K AIFF, 2.2M)

In an alternate universe, Leland Stanford Junior is saved from Typhoid fever in 1884 by a mysterious visitor. Back in 1994, Old Leland encounters the time-traveller, one Gordy Schmegmeir from Cal, and sings this gospel number celebrating his "Second Coming".

Soloists in order: Paul D'Albora '96 as Old Leland, Dave Barlin '95 as Gordy, and Denise Shepherd '97, Liz Brimhall '95, and Salma Qarnain '96 as the gospel singers.
