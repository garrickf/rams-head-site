  • Big Game Gaieties 1996: Admission: Impossible

    • Synopsis: Cal prospective frosh Jordan Ashley has been kidnapped by Dr. KAL as part of some evil plan. He will replace Ashley Jordan in the Stanford class of 2000, and his mission is to steal Dean Montoya's briefcase. Montoya has been kidnapped, and Samantha Freelove, an admissions office employee, hires private "dick" Sterling Quad to track him down. Can Quad, Freelove, Ashley, and ex-child star Blaze O'Glory track down Montoya, stop Dr. KAL's evil plot and convince President Casper and Dean Montoya to do the Macarena? Only in Gaieties!

  • Big Game Gaieties 1995: The Axe Files

    • Synopsis: Ninja-dollie KaNDeE and wannabe grunge king Jeremy meet up with innocent frosh Myra and her RF Professor Hobart at the Mausoleum party. They enter the steam tunnels and discover THE AXE FILES, hidden repository of all the secrets you could ever want to know about Stanford. Together, they uncover a plot to Suck The Fun out of Stanford forever. Can they stop the SUATF before it's too late? Features Jim Montoya as the Devil and the infamous "Meta-Gaieties" scene.

  • Big Game Gaieties 1994: Natural Born Gaieties

    • Synopsis: Sam and Lydia go back in time with Cal nerd Gordy in tow. They accidentally save Leland Stanford Junior from typhoid fever and return to 1994 only to discover Stanford is now Le-Land, an amusement park run by the 126-year-old Leland for the amusement of Cal students. They must return to 1884 and ensure Leland dies on schedule! Includes the disco number "Typhoid Fever!"
