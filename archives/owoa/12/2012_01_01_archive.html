Monday, January 2, 2012

The Original Winter One Acts 2012 are coming!!

Ram's Head presents three new student-written plays
---
January 19 - 21: 8 pm 
Pigott Theater
---
Tickets available at tickets.stanford.edu,  the Stanford Ticket Office in Tresidder Union,
 in White Plaza, and at the door



SPARKLE TIME
by Samantha Toh
directed by Allison Gold
Hilarity ensues when Anne is forced to conceal her affair with flamboyant yoga instructor Gregorio. With her husband and sister on the scene, Anne dashes madly to protect her sanctity amidst sparkly tights, women's underpants and her dead Aunt Hattie.

THE DAYS
by Ashley Chang
directed by Savannah Greene
The story of Rod and Tanya is a disintegrated romance with a happy ending--but only because it's told in reverse.

PLAYING CO-OP
by Patrick Kelly
directed by Harry Spitzer
Lisa and Roy are geeky best friends both struggling with lackluster love lives.  When attractive nerdy girl Melanie moves into their dorm, the two create a competition to see who is man--or woman--enough to win her heart first.
