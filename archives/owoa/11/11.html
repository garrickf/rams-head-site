Thursday, January 6, 2011

The Original Winter One Acts 2011 are coming!!

Ram's Head present three new student-written plays
---
January 13-15: 8 pm
Pigott Theater
---
Tickets available at tickets.stanford.edu,
the Stanford Ticket Office in Tresidder Union, in White Plaza, and at the door



Dear ChickFlickChick
written and directed by Olivia Haas 
College roommates and best friends Sarah and Kelsey live in a world
colored by movie magic mentalities.  Kelsey convinces a hesitant Sarah
that her feelings for her best guy friend, Matt, and his actions towards her
are a sign that they're meant to be together.  After all, life is a
romantic comedy... right?

The Safety
written by Rodrigo Pena and directed by Patrick Kelly 
An unsuspecting robber finds himself holding a surprise party hostage,
and the only one who knows the gun's safety is on is the
unconscious owner of the apartment.

Fix It
written by Samantha Toh and directed by Morielle Stroethoff
Something is not quite right in the household of Molly and Scott Tucker.
Molly longs for a life of stability and happiness that she once had with her husband.
Yet, the only solace she can find comes through reenacting memories
with another man, Buck, a student at a local college
looking for a little extra money on the side.

Tuesday, September 14, 2010

About OWOA


 
 Every January, we premiere two to four student written one acts in a full scale production.  We are going to be looking for playwrights, directors, actors, designers, and technicians to make this wonderful tradition happen!  Playwrights, we will be asking for submissions of 15-40 minute long one acts in early October, so get writing!  Director and designers will be hired in October as well, and auditions will happen in mid-quarter.  If you have any questions, contact producer Heidi Thorsen (thorsen@stanford.edu).
